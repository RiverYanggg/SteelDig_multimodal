The line plots show the fraction distribution of cell sizes measured from ECC (Electron Channeling Contrast Imaging) images. The red curve represents the uncharged sample with an average dislocation cell size of $\\bar{D}_{DC} = 220 \\pm 10$ nm, while the blue curve represents the hydrogen-charged sample (101 mass ppm H, charged at 100 MPa H$_2$ and 543 K for 200 h) with a refined average size of $\\bar{D}_{DC}^H = 192 \\pm 10$ nm.
