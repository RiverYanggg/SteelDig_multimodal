  "title": "The influence of silicon and aluminum alloying on the lattice parameter and stacking fault energy of austenitic steel",
  "abstract_summary": "This study investigates the influence of 2.5 wt.% Si and Al additions on the lattice parameter and stacking fault energy (SFE) of austenitic steels. X-ray diffraction indicates that Al increases the austenite lattice parameter while Si has little effect, which may lead to overestimation of carbon content. Weak-beam dark-field TEM measurements show that Al increases SFE by 12–17 mJ/m² and Si decreases SFE by 6–7 mJ/m², contributing to understanding of alloying effects on austenite stability.",
