Panels (b)-(d) display bright-field TEM micrographs with corresponding selected area electron diffraction (SAED) patterns along the [001] zone axis, illustrating the precipitation evolution of κ-carbides. Specifically, (b) shows the 550-6 sample (aged at 550°C for 6 h) containing uniformly dispersed nanoscale intragranular κ-carbides (~11 nm) within the matrix, with the SAED pattern confirming the L12 ordered structure of κ-phase coexisting with γ. The diffraction patterns superimpose γ and κ reflections, verifying the cube-on-cube orientation relationship.
