Panel (c) demonstrates the 90% CR condition (scale bar 5 μm), characterized by extensive red and green coloring indicating significantly higher misorientation angles distributed throughout the fibrous, nanocrystalline structure with rolling bands. Panel (d) provides quantitative histograms comparing the frequency distributions of average misorientation angles across the three conditions, showing a progressive shift from sharp low-angle peaks in the undeformed state toward broader distributions extending to 5° in the 90% CR sample.
