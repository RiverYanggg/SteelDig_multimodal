  "abstract_summary": "This study examines austenite decomposition reactions in Fe–Mn–Al–C lightweight steels after tempering at 600 °C and 800 °C across compositions with Al 9–12 wt%, Mn 10–30 wt%, and C 0.4–1 wt%. Four distinct transformations—discontinuous precipitation, precipitation transformation, cellular transformation, and spinodal decomposition—were identified and characterized through optical/SEM microscopy, SEM-EDS, and Vickers hardness. Limiting conditions in terms of chemical driving force, thermal energy input, and austenite stability were established, showing discontinuous precipitation at 600 °C in medium-Mn, high-Al, high-C alloys and spinodal decomposition at 800 °C in higher-Mn grades.",
