  "title": "Effect of cold rolling and subsequent heat treatment on microstructural evolution and mechanical properties of Fe-Mn-Al-C-(Ni) based austenitic low-density steels",
  "abstract_summary": "This study investigates the effects of alloy composition (varying Mn and Ni content), 50% cold rolling, and subsequent heat treatment at 973–1173 K on concurrent precipitation of κ-carbide and B2 particles, recrystallization, and mechanical properties in Fe-Mn-Al-C-(Ni) low-density steels. Three alloys—Fe-28Mn-9Al-0.9C, Fe-28Mn-9Al-0.9C-5Ni, and Fe-15Mn-9Al-0.9C-5Ni—were processed to understand the Mn/Ni ratio influence. Results show Ni delays recrystallization; all alloys had similar strength after cold reduction, but heat treatment led to precipitation of B2 and κ-carbides affecting strength and ductility. Enhanced strain hardening in fully recrystallized austenite was attributed to planar slip, dynamic slip band refinement, and dislocation-twin interactions.",
