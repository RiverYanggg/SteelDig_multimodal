The as-quenched microstructure is fully austenitic with equiaxed grains and a high density of Σ3 annealing twins. SEM (Fig. 2) revealed (Nb,Mo,W)C MC carbides (micron-sized polygonal, 1–25 μm; submicron spherical, ~260 nm, ~0.2 vol%). TEM (Figs. 3,4) confirmed their FCC structure with a=4.36 Å and identified nano-sized κ-carbides (L′12) with sizes ~0.8 nm and volume fractions ~3.3–3.9%. The density was 6.82 g/cm^3, ~13% lighter than pure iron. Mechanical property data (tensile and impact) and deformation substructure evolution have not yet been presented but are expected in subsequent chunks.
