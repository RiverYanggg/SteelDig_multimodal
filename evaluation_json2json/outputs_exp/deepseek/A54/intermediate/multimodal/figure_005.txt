The plot displays weight percent (wt%) of Fe (upper curve) and Al (lower curve) as functions of distance from the surface (0–100 μm). Within the oxide region (approximately 0–55 μm), the Fe content exhibits fluctuations between 43 wt% and 67.2 wt%, while Al shows a peak of 14.5 wt% at around 40 μm. In the alloy substrate (beyond ~60 μm), the composition stabilizes at 91.8 wt% Fe and 7.5 wt% Al. The profile reveals a three-layer oxide structure: an outer Fe₂O₃ layer (Fe-rich, Al-depleted), a middle (FeₓAl₁₋ₓ)₂O₃ layer (intermediate Fe and Al), and an inner zone containing internal aluminum oxide precipitates.
