The ST sample exhibits the lowest yield strength (~337 MPa) and ultimate tensile strength (~784 MPa) but superior ductility with total elongation of ~72%. Notably, the 600-6 condition demonstrates the highest tensile strength. Conversely, the 650-6 sample shows diminished strength (YS ~579 MPa, UTS ~978 MPa) and lowest ductility (~52.7% elongation).
