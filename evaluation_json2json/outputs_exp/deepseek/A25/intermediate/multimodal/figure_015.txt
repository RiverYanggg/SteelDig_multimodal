The x-axis displays the three measured elements, while grouped bars represent each alloy composition. The data reveals that Mn content remains relatively constant across all three alloys at approximately 36.2–36.8 at.%, and Cu content stays near 2.6–2.8 at.%. In contrast, Al content in the β-Mn phase increases systematically with nominal alloy composition, rising from 14.3 at.% in 15Al to 16.3 at.% in 17Al and 17.5 at.% in 19Al. These results demonstrate that higher Al additions lead to increased Al dissolution into the β-Mn phase.
