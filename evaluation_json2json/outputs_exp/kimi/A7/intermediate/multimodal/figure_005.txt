Panels (a1) and (a2) display the M1-HR microstructure consisting of polygonal α-ferrite and lath-type martensitic-austenitic (α′+γ) phases, with (a2) providing a higher-magnification view of the lath morphology. Panel (c) compares XRD profiles of both samples, confirming the presence of ferrite/martensite and austenite; quantitative analysis indicates austenite fractions of 5.1 % for M1-HR and 5.7 % for M2-HR.
