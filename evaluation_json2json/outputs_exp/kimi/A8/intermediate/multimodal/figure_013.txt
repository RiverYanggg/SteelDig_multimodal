Panel (a) is a bright-field (BF) micrograph showing a thin κ-carbide precipitate (labeled κ) along an α/α grain boundary within the α matrix (labeled α), with a scale bar of 200 nm. Panel (b) shows the selected-area diffraction pattern (SADP) of α and κ together with an indexed schematic illustrating the Nishiyama–Wasserman (N–W) orientation relationship: [001]α ∥ [011]κ and (110)α ∥ (11̄1)κ. As stated in the caption and text, the partial contrast in the DF image indicates large misorientation between neighboring κ-carbide particles.
