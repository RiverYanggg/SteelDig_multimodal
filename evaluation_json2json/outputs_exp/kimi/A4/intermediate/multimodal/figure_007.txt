With increasing rolling reduction, the ductile fracture character is retained, indicating that toughness is barely compromised despite progressive grain refinement with increased rolling reduction. Notably, the dimple size in HR45 is visibly larger than in HR68 and HR82, which the caption attributes to the larger mean grain size of the lower-reduction sample.
