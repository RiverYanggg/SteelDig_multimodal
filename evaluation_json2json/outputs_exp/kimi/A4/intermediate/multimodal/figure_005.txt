Panels (a–c) show selected-area electron diffraction (SAED) patterns taken from the austenite matrix of the HR45, HR68, and HR82 samples along the [1 0 0] zone axis, respectively, where faint (0 0 1) and (0 1 0) superlattice reflections (marked with circles) indicate the presence of ordered κ-carbides.
