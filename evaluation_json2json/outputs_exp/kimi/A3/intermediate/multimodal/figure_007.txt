Bright-field images show the austenite matrix and intergranular B2 particles (solid white arrows), while dark-field images captured from the (010)κ satellite spot highlight κ-carbide distributions (dotted orange arrows). Recrystallized austenite regions contain fine uniform intragranular κ-carbides, whereas deformed regions contain inhomogeneous coarser κ-carbides of 10–60 nm. The 15Mn5Ni alloy exhibits larger κ-carbides than 28Mn0Ni and 28Mn5Ni under identical conditions. Additionally, Ni-containing alloys display fine intragranular B2 precipitates (5–20 nm, broken yellow arrows) and intergranular B2 (50–200 nm).
