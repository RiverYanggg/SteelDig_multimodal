Panels (a) and (b) display the lattice strain of individual crystallographic reflections—FCC (111), (200), (220), (311), (222) for austenite and BCC (110), (200), (211), (220) for ferrite—as a function of applied engineering strain; the differing initial slopes reflect elastic anisotropy.
