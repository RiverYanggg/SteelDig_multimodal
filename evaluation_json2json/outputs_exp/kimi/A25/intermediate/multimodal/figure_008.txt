  "description": "Figure 7 presents secondary electron (SE) images showing the microstructural evolution of the 15Al alloy (Fe-Mn-Al-Cu) after isothermal aging at 525–650 °C for 1–10 h. The 16 panels collectively document the precipitation sequence and phase transformation from the α matrix to β-Mn. At 525 °C, fine α-Mn particles appear after 1 h, while short rod-shaped β-Mn precipitates along α grain boundaries and interiors become visible by 3 h alongside untransformed α; by 6 h the microstructure is dominated by β-Mn, and new precipitates emerge after 10 h. Similar trends at 550 °C and 575 °C show rapid β-Mn formation within 1–3 h, with complete transformation and subsequent new precipitation at longer times. At higher temperatures (600–625 °C), β-Mn forms even after 1 h, whereas at 650 °C/1 h new precipitates appear immediately. These micrographs demonstrate that the 15Al alloy exhibits fast β-Mn precipitation kinetics, with the α matrix almost fully transformed within 3 h at 525 °C, supporting the conclusion that ultrahigh hardness originates from extensive β-Mn formation.",
