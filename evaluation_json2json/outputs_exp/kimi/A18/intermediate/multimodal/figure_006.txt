Subfigures (a)–(d) are EBSD boundary maps (200 μm scale bars) displaying austenite grains, high-angle grain boundaries (black lines, θ > 15°), Σ3 twin boundaries (red lines), and BCC ferrite regions (green). The average κ-carbide size decreases markedly with increasing Mn content, from about 18 nm in the 20%Mn sample (e) to roughly 5 nm (f), 3 nm (g), and 2 nm (h) for 25%, 30%, and 35% Mn, respectively, as reported in the text.
