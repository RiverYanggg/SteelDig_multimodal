The 0.6%C alloy (a) exhibits a duplex microstructure of FCC austenite and about 30% BCC ferrite, while the 1.0%C (b), 1.2%C (c), and 1.5%C (d) alloys are single-phase austenite with coarser grains. Inset SAED patterns confirm the (001)κ || (001)γ orientation relationship.
