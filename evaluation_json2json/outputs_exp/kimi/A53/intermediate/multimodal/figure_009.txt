The image reveals several nodules situated along the interface between the upper dark region and the lower light-colored alloy substrate, with a 7 μm scale bar. The caption and accompanying text report that these nodules represent the small-nodule morphology typical of alloys containing more than ~7.5% Mn, marking a transition from large iron-rich nodules. The reported nodule structure comprises a grey manganese oxide (Mn₂O₃) layer at the gas–oxide interface overlying a white Fe₂O₃ layer, which contains particles of an aluminum-rich phase and is underlain by a continuous aluminum-rich layer at the nodule–alloy interface.
