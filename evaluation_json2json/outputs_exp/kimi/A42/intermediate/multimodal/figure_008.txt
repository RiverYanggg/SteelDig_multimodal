Subfigures (a)–(d) correspond to MAF-0 (solutionized and quenched), MAF-1, MAF-3, and MAF-5, respectively, with green and red lines denoting low-angle grain boundaries (LAGBs, 1°–15°) and high-angle grain boundaries (HAGBs, 15°–180°). The IPF maps (e–h) reveal that MAF-0 exhibits a strong texture with significant grains having [101] and [111] directions parallel to the pressing direction, whereas increasing MAF passes progressively randomize the crystallographic orientation.
