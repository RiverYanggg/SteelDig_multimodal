Four conditions are compared: M1-SS and M2-SS in the solution-treated state, and M1-30 and M2-30 after aging at 550 °C for 30 h. After aging, M2-30 combines σ_Or from (Ti,V)C with a reduced but significant σ_S (~220 MPa) from κ-carbides precipitated heterogeneously at (Ti,V)C edges, achieving a total yield strength comparable to M1-30 while maintaining a distinct precipitation architecture.
