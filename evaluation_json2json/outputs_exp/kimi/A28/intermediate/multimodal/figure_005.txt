Subfigure (a) shows a bright-field TEM image of the sample deformed at −196 °C, where mechanical twins and dislocations are visible within the austenite matrix; the inset provides the corresponding SAED pattern indexed with a [1,1,0] zone axis, confirming the twinning orientation relationship.
