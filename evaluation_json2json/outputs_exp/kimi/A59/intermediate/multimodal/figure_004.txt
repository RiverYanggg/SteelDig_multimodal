The as-forged baseline (a–c) displays coarse δ-ferrite with interspersed martensite/austenite (M/A) constituents and no lamellar ordering. After low-temperature rolling at 650 °C (R650; d–f), a distinct lamellar architecture forms, comprising δ-ferrite bands alternating with carbide/ferrite composite lamellae that contain ultra-fine spherical carbides averaging 158.3 ± 76.9 nm.
