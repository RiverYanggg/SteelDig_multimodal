Dark-field TEM images (a, e, i) reveal coherent nanoscale κ-carbide particles (3–10 nm) within the austenite matrix, indicated by white arrows. In S1000, bright-field TEM (c) shows elongated stripe-like Cr7C3 precipitates along austenite grain boundaries, indexed by SAED along [1-12]Cr7C3 (d). In S1050, grain boundary Cr7C3 dissolves, and bright-field TEM (g) captures B2 phase particles (20–160 nm), with SAED along [1-11]B2 (h). MHT samples similarly contain B2 (k), slightly smaller than in S1050, verified by SAED along [113]B2 (l).
