Subfigure (a) displays five curves for Fe-15Al-based alloys: binary Fe-15Al (1), Fe-15Al-2Ti-1C (2), Fe-15Al-2V-1C (3), Fe-15Al-2Nb-1C (4), and Fe-15Al-2Ta-1C (5). Nb- and Ta-bearing compositions reach the highest stress levels but display almost no tensile plasticity.
