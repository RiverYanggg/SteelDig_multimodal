Subfigures (c,d) correspond to the 28Mn5Ni alloy, and (e,f) correspond to the 15Mn5Ni alloy; both Ni-containing alloys exhibit B2 intermetallic precipitates (marked by arrows) distributed in the austenite matrix. The fraction of intragranular B2 precipitates decreases as the recrystallized austenite fraction increases, verifying that highly strained deformed regions are preferential nucleation sites for the B2 phase.
