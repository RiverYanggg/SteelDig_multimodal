Subfigures (a,c,e,g) are bright-field TEM images, while (b,d,f,h) are dark-field TEM images captured from the (010) κ-carbide satellite spot in selected area electron diffraction (SAED) patterns, with insets of a and c showing SAED patterns under [101] zone axis of austenite grains. Finer uniformly distributed κ-carbides exist in recrystallized austenite regions, while coarser inhomogeneous κ-carbides are observed in deformed austenite regions. Ni-containing alloys show both intergranular and intragranular B2 precipitates, while Ni-free 28Mn0Ni only presents intergranular B2.
