Subfigures (a)-(f) correspond to 10Al steel, while (g)-(l) correspond to 8Al steel. Each subfigure includes a selected area electron diffraction (SAED) pattern at the upper right, indicating the operating diffraction vector g and [111] zone axis, with a 500 nm scale bar for the TEM view. g·b analysis confirms the Burgers vector of dislocations in ferrite of both steels is a/2<111>. For 10Al steel, ferrite dislocations are dominated by long straight screw dislocations with non-uniform distribution. For 8Al duplex ferrite-austenite steel, screw, edge, and mixed dislocations all exist with planar slip feature, and edge dislocations are preferentially activated at α/γ phase boundaries.
