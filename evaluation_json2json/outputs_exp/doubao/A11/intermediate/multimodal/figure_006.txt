The inputs include three categories: load conditions (temperatures, strain rates of two deformation stages, start strain of the second stage, end strain), initial microstructure (grain orientation, grain size, dislocation density, etc.), and model parameters (initial work hardening rate, saturation stress, DRX activation energy, plus constitutive calibrated parameters). It further judges if dislocation density reaches the critical value to trigger inhomogeneous DRX nucleation, updates nucleation site parameters, calculates grain boundary migration, updates cell state variables, and stops when reaching the last step to output results including grain orientation distribution and dislocation density distribution.
