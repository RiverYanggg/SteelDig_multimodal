Possible composition candidates first enter the filtering module integrated with machine learning-based prediction models, and go through 5 sequential judgment steps: checking whether κ carbide exists, whether a single γ austenite region exists, whether (γ+κ) dual-phase region appears at 500-600℃, whether κ phase fraction is high enough, and whether the material has low density.
