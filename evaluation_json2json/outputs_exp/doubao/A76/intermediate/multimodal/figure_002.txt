Each steel variant is characterized in as-cast state, and after 3 h aging treatment at 723 K, 773 K, 823 K respectively, with a uniform scale bar of 20 μm for all subfigures. Ferrite volume fraction of both steels ranges from ~16 vol% to 18.4 vol%, slightly increasing with rising aging temperature, and shows no significant dependence on Nb content. Higher Nb addition leads to more NbC precipitates and finer austenite grains in 0.04Nb steel.
