Subfigures a/e correspond to solution-treated (ST) samples, b/f to samples aged at 550 °C for 6 h (550-6), c/g to samples aged at 600 °C for 6 h (600-6), d/h to samples aged at 650 °C for 6 h (650-6). Obvious morphology differences are observed: fatigue striation spacing is larger under 315 MPa than 180 MPa; the 650-6 sample with severe intergranular κ-carbide precipitation shows partial intergranular fracture features, while the 600-6 sample has the finest striation spacing at 180 MPa, consistent with its highest fatigue limit.
