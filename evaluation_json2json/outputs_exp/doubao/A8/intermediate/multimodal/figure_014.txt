For the 800 °C annealed specimen, three phases (α-ferrite, γ-austenite, κ-carbide) are observed, and EDS line scans show that κ-carbide has the highest Mn and Al contents among the three phases, while α phase has higher Al content but lower Mn content than γ phase.
