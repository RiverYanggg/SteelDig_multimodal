    "description": "This is an energy dispersive spectroscopy (EDS) elemental X-ray map (part of the Fig. 5 series) for the cross-section of oxide scale formed on Fe–7.5Mn–5Al alloy after isothermal high-temperature oxidation under 200 torr pure oxygen. As reported in the associated text, the map demonstrates significant enrichment of Mn and Al both in the inner Fe₃O₄ layer of the oxide nodules and at the gas-oxide interface. For alloys containing more than ~5 wt% Mn, the observed Mn enrichment at the gas-oxide interface corresponds to a thin, semi-continuous outer grey oxide layer. This elemental distribution result confirms the Mn and Al migration behavior during oxidation, and supports the conclusion that Mn concentration is a key factor determining the phase composition and structural characteristics of oxide scales formed on Fe-Mn-Al alloy systems.",
