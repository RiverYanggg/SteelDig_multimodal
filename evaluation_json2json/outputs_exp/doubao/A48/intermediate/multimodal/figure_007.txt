Three categories of performance are plotted: 1) Mechanical strength: ultimate tensile strength (UTS, solid triangles) and 0.2% yield strength (YS, hollow triangles) both peak at ~1200 MPa and ~1020 MPa respectively in the Mn range of 26-29 wt%, then decrease gradually as Mn rises, dropping to ~950 MPa and ~880 MPa at 34 wt% Mn. 2) Ductility: reduction of area (ψ, hollow circles) and elongation (δ, solid circles) reach their maximum values of ~60% and ~44% around 30 wt% Mn, then drop sharply when Mn exceeds 32 wt%, to ~22% and ~11% at 34 wt% Mn respectively. 3) U-notch impact toughness (K_CU, hollow rhombuses) maintains at 100-115 J/cm² when Mn is 24-32 wt%, then plummets to ~30 J/cm² at 34 wt% Mn.
