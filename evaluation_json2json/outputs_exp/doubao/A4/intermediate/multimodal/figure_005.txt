Subfigures (a-c) are selected area electron diffraction (SAED) patterns along [100] zone axis taken from austenite matrix of HR45 (45% reduction, a&d), HR68 (68% reduction, b&e) and HR82 (82% reduction, c&f) samples, with a scale bar of 5 1/nm.
