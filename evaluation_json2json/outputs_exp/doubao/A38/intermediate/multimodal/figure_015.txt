The P-70 sample shows abundant unfused spherical powder particles, lack of fusion defects, and obvious cracks, corresponding to poor tensile performance. Fracture surfaces of P-90 and P-110 are dominated by fine dimples, indicative of superior ductility, yield strength and tensile strength, representing the optimal processing window. For P-130 and P-150, cleavage planes, internal cracks, and spheroidization defects appear alongside dimples, leading to deteriorated mechanical properties.
