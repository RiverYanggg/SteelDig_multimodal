Subfigures (a) to (e) show transverse cross-sectional grain size distributions of samples prepared at laser powers of 70 W (P-70), 90 W (P-90), 110 W (P-110), 130 W (P-130), and 150 W (P-150), respectively, while subfigure (f) displays the longitudinal grain size distribution of the P-110 sample. A clear trend is observed: as laser power increases from 70 W to 150 W, both the average transverse grain size and the fraction of large grains continuously increase. The larger longitudinal grain size of P-110 is attributed to faster cooling rate in the horizontal direction than the vertical direction during SLM solidification.
