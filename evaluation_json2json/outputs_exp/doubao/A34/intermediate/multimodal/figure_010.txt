DRX grain size increases with elevated temperature or reduced strain rate: under low temperature (900–950 °C) and high strain rate (1–10 s⁻¹), DRX is incomplete with residual coarse original grains and poor homogeneity, while high temperature/low strain rate produces coarser fully recrystallized grains.
