It shows microstructure evolution with increasing applied strain: initially, three phases exist: γ_c (austenite that directly undergoes TRIP to form α' martensite), α ferrite, and γ_f (austenite that experiences dislocation glide before TRIP transformation, with red arrows representing Mn partitioning from ferrite to austenite). At higher strain, γ_f also transforms to martensite via the TRIP effect.
